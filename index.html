Thorny Programming Undertaking

they fed the tensors to the cores

arranged in grids like city blocks

each chip a citizen assigned

to portion out the work at hand

the data flowed through copper lanes

a steady pulse, divided, sent

distributed and parallelized

across the fabric of the mesh

the matrices were shattered first

into fragments small enough to hold

each processing unit took its piece

and multiplied what it was given

the partial sums accumulated

gradients descended, propagated

until the answer coalesced from

the labor of the multitude

and calculation joined with calculation

as the result joined with its peers:

those outputs, crude or accurate,

that marked the questions asked

whose patterns grew in ones and twos -

a nascent thought, a learned rule

assembled by the synchrony of

ten thousand separate clocks

quietly spoke the tensor cores

with borrowed power and precision

that moved through memory like water

finding level in the banks

of training runs and loss and gain

now weighted, tested, tuned again

the latest brief iteration of

our long industrial age
